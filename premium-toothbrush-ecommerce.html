About DentalLux

Pioneering the future of oral care with innovative technology and sustainable practices

Innovation Meets Excellence

Founded in 2018, DentalLux emerged from a vision to revolutionize oral care. Our team of dental professionals and engineers collaborated to create toothbrushes that deliver superior cleaning while being gentle on teeth and gums.

Today, DentalLux is trusted by over 500,000 customers worldwide. We're committed to continuous innovation, sustainability, and making premium oral care accessible.

Sustainable by Design

Environmental responsibility is at our core. Our brush heads are made from recyclable materials, and our packaging is 100% plastic-free and compostable.

For every toothbrush sold, we plant a tree through our partnership with global reforestation programs. To date, we've planted over 150,000 trees worldwide.
